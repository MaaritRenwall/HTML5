<h3>Description</h3>
<p>The Spanish Water Dog (perro de agua espa√±ol) breed is used in Spain as a general purpose sheepdog and guard. It is also used sometimes as a gundog, and is skilled at retrieval from water.The Spanish Water Dog (SWD) has strong genetic links to other water breeds such as the Portuguese Water Dog, The Poodle, the French Barbet and the Irish Water Spaniel.</p>
